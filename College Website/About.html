About Us

Established in 1979, Sri Dhavala College has grown into a prominent institution of higher education in Moodubidire. True to its motto, "Arivininda Arahanta", it seeks to enlighten the community and spread the light of knowledge far and wide.

The college is located on a spacious and serene campus, away from the hustle and bustle of the town. The College caters to the educational needs of a wide range of students, especially those belonging to low socio-economic groups. Adequate emphasis is given to the education of girls from rural areas.
