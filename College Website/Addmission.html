ADMISSION GUIDELINES

Eligibility for Admission

A pass in two year P.U.C. or its equivalent with all relevant subjects

Intake capacity of the Programmes

Programme Capacity
B.Com 90
B.C.A. 60

Admission Guidelines

  1. Admission to any course is for one academic year only. All students should seek re-admission after the first year.
  2. Candidates are required to apply for admission in the prescribed form available in the college office.
  3. Applications completed in all respects along with relevant documents should reach the principal on or before the last date notified.
  4. Candidates appearing for interview should bring the following certificates/documents in original:
    • II P.U.C. and S.S.L.C. marks card
    • Transfer Certificate and conduct certificates from institution last attended.
    • Three copies of the recent passport size photographs of the candidate.
    • Cast and Income Certificate for SC/ST students.
    • Migration eligibility certificate, if the applicant is coming from other University/State.
  5. The applicants are required to bring their parents/guardians when they appear for interview
  6. Admissions to the college are provisional and subject to the approval of the University.
