About Our Institution

Sri Dhavala College was established in 1965 by the Sri Dhavala Education Trust with the objective of providing education to students from rural and disadvantaged backgrounds in and around Moodabidre. The college aims to foster character development and instill a strong sense of discipline among its students. Today, the college is recognized as one of the premier pre-university colleges in the state, renowned for its commitment to delivering quality education and nurturing outstanding academic performance among its students.
